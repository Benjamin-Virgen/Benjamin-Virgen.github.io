The Delectable Review: Ratatouille

The Appetizer

If you haven't been living in an elderly woman's ceiling unbeknownst to her, then you have probably watched or heard of the award-winning animated Pixar film, Ratatouille.


A charming film that touched the hearts of many, Ratatouille quickly became one of the most beloved films within Pixar's repertoire. It is packed full of flavor: from powerful emotion, to amazing comedy, and a heartfelt message: we are all capable of seeing victory, despite being from different backgrounds that may restrict our oppoprtunity.
